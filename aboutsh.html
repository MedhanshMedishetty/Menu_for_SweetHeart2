About Us

Welcome to Sweet Heart Restaurant 2, where tradition meets flavor in the heart of Hyderabad. Since our inception, we’ve been passionate about preserving the legacy of authentic, oven‑fresh bakery specialties, aromatic Hyderabadi biryanis, and comforting mugs of Irani tea. As one of the select few establishments keeping traditional baking alive, we take pride in every loaf and pastry that leaves our oven.

With our roots firmly grounded in the community, we’re open 24 hours to cater to your cravings—any time of day or night. Located in Muthangi, Patancheru, our welcoming atmosphere and rich culinary traditions make us a beloved destination for both locals and visitors alike.

Whether you're enjoying our signature dum biryani, indulging in bakery delights straight from the oven, or sipping on our renowned Irani tea, Sweet Heart Restaurant 2 is where flavor, heritage, and warmth come together.
